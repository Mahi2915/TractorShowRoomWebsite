Mahindra YUVRAJ 215 NXT NT Tractor

The Mahindra YUVRAJ 215 NXT NT Tractor is a powerful and efficient tractor that is ideal for intercultural operations due to its narrow track width (711 mm). Equipped with a 10.4 kW (15 HP) engine, this tractor delivers high fuel efficiency, making it a cost-effective choice for farmers. The YUVRAJ 215 NXT NT Tractor is efficient in cultivation, rotavating, and spraying. It has a wide range of gears in segments that allow it to perform a variety of tasks, and its high ground clearance makes it ideal for working in uneven terrain. The tractor also has a 778 kg lifting capacity, making it easy to carry heavy loads.
