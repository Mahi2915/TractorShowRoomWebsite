Mahindra ARJUN 605 DI PP Tractor

The Mahindra ARJUN 605 DI PP Tractors are durable and high-performing machines equipped with advanced mBoost technology, which has the potential to transform your farm operations. These Mahindra tractors provide a powerful 44.8 kW (60 HP) engine, power steering, and a hydraulics lifting capacity of 1800 kg. If you seek an ARJUN tractor that excels in a wide range of powerful and precise farming tasks, the Mahindra ARJUN 605 DI PP Tractor is an indispensable asset.
